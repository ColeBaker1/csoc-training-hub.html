Bowser — AI Triage Assistant

Internal L1 Agent · Confidential
Bowser is Blackford's in-house, next-generation L1 agent — built by SOC analysts, for SOC analysts. It harmonises governance, process, and analysis during alert triage, drawing on a knowledge base of 150+ internal SOPs and playbooks, and connects to Defender using your own permissions (OAuth / RBAC), so you only ever see what you're entitled to see. Use it to speed up triage, stay aligned to playbooks, and close knowledge gaps.
MODULE 01
Query Assistant
Builds and parses KQL for you during an investigation — describe what you need to see and refine from there instead of writing queries from scratch.
MODULE 02
Governance Fetcher
Answers governance, policy, and process questions with pinpoint accuracy from the internal knowledge base — no more digging through SharePoint mid-incident.
MODULE 03
Playbook Tracker
Surfaces the correct playbook for the alert you're working and tracks your progress through it, ensuring complete adherence to pre-defined procedures.
MODULE 04
Defender Connector
Pulls incident and entity context directly from Defender XDR, authenticated with your own RBAC-scoped permissions.
Using Bowser During Alert Triage
Step 01
Open the incident in Defender XDR / Sentinel and ask Bowser for the matching playbook.
Step 02
Use the Governance Fetcher for any process or escalation questions as they arise.
Step 03
Use the Query Assistant to build the KQL you need to gather evidence and enrich entities.
Step 04
Work through the playbook with the Playbook Tracker — verify Bowser's outputs with your own analysis before drawing conclusions.
Step 05
Document findings and disposition per the reporting standards. You own the verdict — Bowser supplements your judgement, it doesn't replace it.
